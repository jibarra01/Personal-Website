SERENDIPITY STICKER

The "Serendipity" project is a graphic design endeavor aimed at creating stickers to accompany a music video. The sticker design was crafted with Adobe Illustrator, drawing inspiration from BTS' "Serendipity" music video. Through its imagery, color palette, and typography, the sticker was tailored to convey specific symbolism from the video. Measuring 2 x 3 inches, the project will yield high-quality, durable, and long-lasting stickers, printed on glossy vinyl material.

 

POSTCARD DESIGN

"The "Landscape" project is a postcard design graphic that effectively communicates a sense of nature and encourages exploration through stunning imagery and bold colors. The design process involved defining the postcard's purpose and message, gathering inspiration, and creating sketches to visualize the concept. The final design, created using Adobe Illustrator, features a vibrant color palette and stunning imagery of scenic landscapes. The postcard was printed on high-quality paper using digital printing methods to ensure the vibrancy and crispness of the design.
